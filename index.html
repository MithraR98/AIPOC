POC Playbook

Intro

This playbook is a practical framework for integrating AI into enterprise product design workflows — governed experimentations, reusable prompting systems, workflow automation, and human-led validation.

Best Use of AI

During this POC, we focused on using mainly two tools — Claude Code and Figma Make — and measured their impact on our workflow by the quality of output produced at different stages.
